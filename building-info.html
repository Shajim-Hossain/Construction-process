Oakwood Residence

The Oakwood Residence is a modern two-story family home currently under construction in the scenic Hillview neighborhood. This elegant residential property spans 2,500 square feet of thoughtfully designed living space, featuring 4 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms, and an open-concept main floor plan that seamlessly integrates the kitchen, dining, and living areas.

The architecture combines contemporary design elements with traditional craftsmanship, showcasing large energy-efficient windows that flood the interior with natural light, vaulted ceilings in the main living area, and a covered porch that wraps around the front and side of the house. The exterior features a harmonious blend of stone veneer and fiber cement siding, complemented by architectural shingles and decorative trim work.

Construction began in January 2025 and is scheduled for completion by August 2025. The foundation has been laid, framing is complete, and teams are currently working on electrical wiring, plumbing installation, and HVAC systems. The next phases will include drywall installation, interior finishing, and landscaping.
